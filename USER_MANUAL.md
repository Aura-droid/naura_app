# Mwandet Digital User Manual

This guide explains how to use the Mwandet Digital system in simple language.

It is written for:
- Teachers
- Academic Office
- Management / Head of School


## 1. What This System Is For

The Mwandet Digital system helps staff do school work online using a phone or computer.

Main uses:
- Record class attendance
- Submit T.O.D. reports
- Fill exam templates sent by the Academic Office
- Review reports and export documents


## 2. Who Uses Which Part

### Teachers
Teachers use the **Teacher Hub** to:
- Record attendance
- Submit T.O.D. reports
- Fill exam templates and marks sent by the Academic Office

### Academic Office
Academic Office users use the **Academic Office Dashboard** to:
- Upload exam templates
- Open templates for teachers
- Track which subjects are still pending
- Export completed Excel files
- Download missing submission reports

### Management / Head of School
Management users use the **Management Dashboard** to:
- See attendance summaries
- Review staff activity
- View reports
- Access Academic Office tools if needed


## 3. Logging In

1. Open the system link.
2. Enter your username.
3. Enter your password.
4. Tap **Log in**.

After login:
- Teachers go to **Teacher Hub**
- Academic Office users go to **Academic Office Dashboard**
- Management users go to **Management Dashboard**


## 4. Teacher Hub

The Teacher Hub has 3 main sections:
- **Class Attendance**
- **T.O.D. Report**
- **Exam Templates**

If there are any exam templates still waiting for submission, you may see a yellow reminder box at the top.


## 5. How To Record Attendance

1. Open **Teacher Hub**.
2. Tap **Class Attendance**.
3. Enter the class details.
4. Fill the numbers correctly.
5. Check that totals match.
6. Tap save/submit.

Use this for daily attendance records.


## 6. How To Submit a T.O.D. Report

1. Open **Teacher Hub**.
2. Tap **T.O.D. Report**.
3. Fill all the required fields.
4. Check your times and comments.
5. Submit the report.

Use this when you are the teacher on duty or responsible for the report.


## 7. How To Fill Exam Templates

1. Open **Teacher Hub**.
2. Tap **Exam Templates**.
3. Choose the open template you want.
4. Choose your subject.
5. Enter marks for the candidate numbers shown.
6. When finished, tap **Confirm and Send**.

Important:
- Candidate number is the main reference on the form.
- Teachers can save drafts while working.
- Teachers can still edit while the template is open.
- When Academic Office closes the template, editing stops.


## 8. Search On the Marks Page

The marks page has a search box.

Use it when:
- You want to jump to a candidate number quickly
- You want to check one learner fast

How to use:
1. Type the candidate number in the search box.
2. The page filters the rows.
3. Clear the search box to show all rows again.


## 9. Autosave

The marks page saves draft work automatically after you type.

This helps when:
- Network is weak
- You are interrupted while entering marks
- You are afraid of losing work

You will see a save message like:
- **Unsaved changes**
- **Saved at 14:20**


## 10. Progress Summary

At the top of the marks page, you can see:
- How many marks are entered
- How many are still blank
- Current status
- Last autosave time

This helps you know how much work is left.


## 11. Blank Highlighting

When you try to submit the final marks:
- Blank boxes are highlighted
- A confirmation message appears

Example:
- “You are about to submit English, 173 filled, 12 blank.”

This helps reduce mistakes.


## 12. Bulk Paste

Bulk paste helps when you already have marks in a list.

Example 1: one score per line

```text
20
31
45
ABS
28
```

If the scores are in the same order as the candidate numbers on the page, the system fills them in order.

Example 2: candidate number and score together

```text
0051 20
0052 31
0053 ABS
0054 45
```

This is useful when:
- marks come from notes
- marks come from WhatsApp
- marks were copied from another list


## 13. Academic Office Dashboard

Academic Office users can:
- Upload a new Excel template
- Open templates for teachers automatically
- See which subjects are:
  - Pending
  - In Progress
  - Submitted
- Export completed Excel files
- Export missing submission reports


## 14. How To Upload a Template

1. Open **Academic Office Dashboard**.
2. Enter the template name.
3. Upload the Excel workbook.
4. Submit/upload it.

New templates open automatically for teachers.


## 15. How To Check Submission Progress

Academic Office can open a template and see:
- Which subjects are submitted
- Which subjects are still pending
- How many students are done
- Who updated a subject last
- When it was updated

This helps follow up with teachers quickly.


## 16. Missing Submissions Report

Academic Office can download a report showing subjects that are not yet fully submitted.

This report can help when:
- reminding teachers
- checking late submissions
- following up before closing the template


## 17. Management Dashboard

Management users can:
- Review attendance data
- View trends and reports
- Check staff activity
- Access Academic Office pages
- Review school status information


## 18. Navigation

The top navigation shows only links your account is allowed to use.

Examples:
- Teachers see Teacher Hub
- Academic Office sees Teacher Hub and Academic Office
- Management sees all lower sections too


## 19. Logging Out

To log out:
1. Tap **Logout**
2. You will return to the login page


## 20. When Something Goes Wrong

### If you cannot log in
- Check your username carefully
- Check your password carefully
- Make sure caps lock is off
- Ask the administrator to confirm your account group

### If you cannot see a template
- It may not be uploaded yet
- It may not be open yet
- Ask Academic Office to confirm it is open

### If you cannot edit marks
- The template may already be closed
- Ask Academic Office to reopen it if changes are still needed

### If the page does not save
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a few seconds and try again
- Refresh the page only after confirming whether work was saved

### If numbers look wrong
- Review the candidate numbers carefully
- Check blanks before final submission
- Use the search box to confirm individual rows

### If export is missing data
- Check whether all subjects are marked as submitted
- Check the missing submissions report


## 21. How To Get Help

If you need help:
- Contact the **Second Master's Office**
- Use the WhatsApp help link shown on the Teacher Hub

Use help when:
- you forgot your password
- a page is not loading
- a template is missing
- marks are not saving
- you do not know which dashboard to use


## 22. Good Practice Tips

- Always check candidate numbers carefully
- Save drafts while working
- Do not wait until the last minute to submit
- Review blank fields before final submission
- Log out after using a shared device


## 23. Short Summary

In simple words:
- Teachers enter daily work and marks
- Academic Office sends templates and monitors submissions
- Management reviews reports and progress
- The system helps everyone work faster using phones or computers
